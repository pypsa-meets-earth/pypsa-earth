# Contributors

The following individuals and organizations were the initial contributors to PyPSA-ASEAN.

## Initial Contributors
- **Arsyan Mohamad Virio Andreyana**: Energy System Modeller and Southeast Asia regional lead at OET. Contributor to the development of PyPSA and its ecosystem. Graduate of TU Berlin.

- **Fillea Fakhira**: A Master’s student in Renewable Energy Systems at TU Berlin and a researcher at Reiner Lemoine Institut on grid integration of renewables in Southeast Asia.

- **Sophia Bernadette Lunor**: A Master’s student in Energy Engineering at UP Diliman, specializing in energy systems modeling and transnational energy transitions in Southeast Asia.

- **Paul Bertheau**: A PhD researcher focused on sustainable energy for Philippine islands, specializing in off-grid planning and renewable energy transitions in Southeast Asia.

## Initial Organization Contributors

- **Open Energy Transition**: The core developers of PyPSA and its ecosystem, supporting work with ENTSO-E, ACER, and national TSOs through model customization, data pipelines, open datasets, and training.

- **Technical University of Berlin**: The Department of Digital Transformation in Energy Systems (ENSYS) developes PyPSA: an open-source energy system model to study renewable integration, sector coupling, and energy system transformation.

- **University of the Philippines Diliman**: The Philippines’ premier national research university, shaping sustainable energy futures through a multidisciplinary and technically advanced Energy Engineering program.

- **Reiner Lemoine Institut**: RLI is an independent, non-profit research institute advancing a 100% renewable energy future through open-source modeling, data-driven analysis, and practical projects in Germany and beyond.

We welcome contributions from individuals and organizations using and extending PyPSA-ASEAN. If you would like to be acknowledged here, please reach us in [Github](https://github.com/pypsa-meets-earth/pypsa-asean) or [Discord: PyPSA-ASEAN](https://discord.gg/AnuJBk23FU).
